Character Tracter

Website

Character Tracker is a web application made to keep track of base statistics in a group of players in a Dungeons and Dragons campaign.

To make this possible, I used Javascript ES6 for the first time. It includes functionality to dynamically add players and their stats to the page, as well as saving and loading them from browser storage.

Tide Background App

Android App

WARNING: DEPRECATED

The Tide Background App is an app I created for my android phone based on the Tide Motivational App. Every morning, I enjoy looking at their daily quote. This app, manually or automatically, will change your phone background to the quote and image that they put on Twitter.

Unfortunately, three days about I finished the working version of the app, the Tide Twitter stopped posting their quotes, rendering this app useless. Still, it is a good example of a basic android app with web scraping.
